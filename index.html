Why Should You Be Concerned?

1 in 4 individuals experience mental health issues globally, affecting productivity and economic stability. Depression and anxiety disorders alone costs the global economy $1 trillion annually. Early intervention is crucial, especially during adolescence and young adulthood when 75% of mental health conditions emerge. Stigma and barriers to care hinders many from seeking support.
